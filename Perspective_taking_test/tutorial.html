Prospective Taking Test Tutorial

Objective

Your task in the Prospective Taking Test is to point towards various objects in the environment based on the given prompt.

How to Play

  1. A prompt will appear at the top of the game area. The prompt will ask you to imagine standing at one object and pointing towards another.
  2. Click anywhere in the circle to draw a line indicating the direction you would point based on the prompt.
  3. Once you're satisfied with your direction, click the "Submit" button to confirm your answer.
  4. The game continues with new prompts until you've answered all of them or time runs out. Try to answer as many prompts as possible within the time limit!

Scoring

Your accuracy in pointing towards the correct object is measured and saved. Try to be as precise as possible!
